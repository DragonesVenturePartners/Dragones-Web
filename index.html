Investment thesis

An investment model with active support.

The traditional VC model bets on a couple of companies with very high multiples — 10x, 50x, 100x — that absorb the many that fall along the way. Our approach is different: a focused portfolio, individual thesis per company and active support from the board. Each investment has to stand on its own and return a minimum of 5x, without depending on one winner to carry the portfolio.

Investment evaluation

What we look at before investing.

We analyze the deal with investor discipline: structure, alignment and returns.

  • Round size and valuation
  • Cap table
  • Ticket, terms and deal structuring
  • Shareholder agreement and ESOP allocation
  • Previous rounds and investors
  • Exit strategy (M&A, IPO)
  • Business plan: projected revenues and EBITDA
  • Potential multiples and timing to exit
What we look for in the startup

Founders above all.

01
Founders and team

Technical skills, soft skills, character, track record, commitment, resilience and enthusiasm. We want talented and open people, willing to listen.

02
Market and barriers

Total addressable market, competition, barriers to entry, potential for patents and IP, scalability and geographic expansion.

03
Clear use of funds

Clear runway, estimated time to next round and well-defined use of proceeds. Sound legal structure of incorporation.
